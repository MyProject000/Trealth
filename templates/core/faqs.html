Frequently Asked Questions

To book an appointment, you need to register or log in to your patient account. Then, you can use the "Find Doctor" feature to search for specialists based on their name, specialization, or location. Select a doctor, choose an available time slot, and confirm your booking.

Yes, we take data security seriously. All personal and medical information is encrypted and stored securely. We comply with healthcare privacy regulations and implement stringent security measures to protect your data. Only authorized healthcare providers can access your medical information when necessary for your treatment.

You can cancel or reschedule an appointment by logging into your account, going to the "My Appointments" section, selecting the appointment you wish to modify, and clicking on "Cancel" or "Reschedule." Please note that some doctors may have specific cancellation policies, which will be displayed during the booking process.

We offer multiple payment options including credit/debit cards and online banking. You can pay for your appointment during the booking process or after your consultation. All payments are processed securely through our encrypted payment gateway.

Doctors can join our platform by registering with a doctor account. You'll need to provide your professional details, including your specialization, license number, and hospital affiliations. Our team will verify your credentials before activating your profile.

As a doctor, you can manage your availability through the "Schedule Management" section of your dashboard. You can set your working hours, block off unavailable times, and specify the maximum number of appointments per day.

If you forgot your password, click on the "Login" button, then select "Forgot Password." Enter the email address associated with your account, and we'll send you a password reset link. Follow the instructions in the email to create a new password.

Yes, our medical appointment system is fully responsive and works on all devices including smartphones, tablets, and desktop computers. You can access all features through your mobile browser without needing to install any apps.
